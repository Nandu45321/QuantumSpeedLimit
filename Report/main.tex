\begin{abstract}
This internship focused on understanding Quantum Speed Limits (QSLs), which define the fundamental limits on the time required for a quantum system to evolve from one state to another. The project explored various formulations of QSLs, including the Mandelstam-Tamm bound and the Margolus-Levitin bound, and their applications in two state quantum systems. 
\end{abstract}
